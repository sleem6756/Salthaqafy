# Privacy Policy for Althaqafy Quran App

**Last Updated**: November 29, 2025

## Introduction

Welcome to Althaqafy ("we," "our," or "us"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you use the Althaqafy Quran mobile application (the "App").

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide
- **No Account Required**: Our App does not require you to create an account or provide personal information.
- **Contact Information**: If you contact us for support, we may collect your name and email address.

### Information Collected Automatically

#### Location Data
- **Purpose**: To determine the Qibla direction for prayer
- **Permission**: We request location permission (ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION, ACCESS_COARSE_LOCATION)
- **Usage**: Location data is used locally on your device and is not stored or transmitted to our servers
- **Control**: You can deny location permission; the Qibla feature will not work without it

#### Storage/Media Access
- **Purpose**: To download and cache Quran audio files for offline playback
- **Permission**: We request storage permission (READ_MEDIA_AUDIO for Android 13+, READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE for older versions)
- **Usage**: Audio files are stored locally on your device for offline access
- **Control**: You can deny storage permission; offline audio playback will not be available

#### Notification Data
- **Purpose**: To display audio playback controls and prayer time reminders
- **Permission**: We request notification permission (POST_NOTIFICATIONS on Android 13+)
- **Usage**: Notifications are displayed locally on your device
- **Control**: You can disable notifications in your device settings

## How We Use Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:
- **Location**: Calculate Qibla direction based on your current location
- **Storage**: Save Quran audio files for offline listening
- **Notifications**: Display audio playback controls and prayer time alerts
- **App Functionality**: Ensure the App works correctly on your device

## Data Sharing and Disclosure

**We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties.**

We may share information only in the following circumstances:
- **Legal Requirements**: If required by law, court order, or governmental authority
- **App Functionality**: Some data (like audio streaming) may be fetched from third-party CDNs or APIs

## Third-Party Services

The App may use third-party services for the following purposes:
- **Quran Audio Streaming**: Audio files may be streamed from third-party servers
- **Quran Text Data**: Quran text and translations are sourced from public Islamic databases

These third-party services have their own privacy policies. We encourage you to review them.

## Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information:
- All location calculations are performed locally on your device
- Downloaded audio files are stored securely on your device
- We use secure HTTPS connections when communicating with servers
- We do not store your personal data on our servers

## Children's Privacy

Our App does not knowingly collect information from children under 13 years of age. The App is designed for general Islamic education and is suitable for all ages with parental guidance.

## Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your information:
- **Location Permission**: You can revoke location permission in your device settings
- **Storage Permission**: You can revoke storage permission in your device settings
- **Notification Permission**: You can disable notifications in your device settings
- **Data Deletion**: You can delete the App and all associated data by uninstalling it from your device

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

## Permissions Summary

### Android Permissions
- `INTERNET` - To stream Quran audio
- `ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE` - To check network connectivity
- `ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION` / `ACCESS_COARSE_LOCATION` - For Qibla direction
- `READ_MEDIA_AUDIO` (Android 13+) - To cache audio files
- `POST_NOTIFICATIONS` (Android 13+) - For playback controls and reminders
- `FOREGROUND_SERVICE` / `FOREGROUND_SERVICE_MEDIA_PLAYBACK` - For background audio playback
- `WAKE_LOCK` - To keep audio playing when screen is off
- `SCHEDULE_EXACT_ALARM` - For precise prayer time notifications
- `RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED` - To restore scheduled notifications after device restart

### iOS Permissions
- `NSLocationWhenInUseUsageDescription` - For Qibla direction
- `NSAppleMusicUsageDescription` - For audio playback
- `UIBackgroundModes (audio)` - For background audio playback

## Data Retention

- **Location Data**: Not stored; calculated in real-time when needed
- **Downloaded Audio**: Stored until you clear app data or uninstall the app
- **Settings/Preferences**: Stored locally until you uninstall the app
